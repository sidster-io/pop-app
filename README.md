## Description
Pop is your essential clipboard manager, designed to streamline your workflow by keeping all your copied content organized and instantly accessible. Whether you’re a student, professional, or creative, Pop ensures that every piece of text, link, or image you copy is just a keystroke away.
With seamless integration across the Apple ecosystem, Pop – Paste, Organize, Perform – lets you manage your clipboard effortlessly on macOS, iOS, iPadOS, and visionOS using iCloud sync. No more re-copying the same things over and over or hunting through apps to find that one snippet you lost.

### Why Choose Pop?

Pop stands out with its clean design, powerful keyboard-driven workflow, and a thoughtful feature set that makes clipboard management feel natural instead of noisy. The Basic version includes the most-used features for free, and when you are ready to go deeper, Pop Pro unlocks advanced tools that supercharge power users without getting in the way.

## Alternatives
As a long-time user of clipboard managers, I never upgraded when Paste moved to a subscription model, so I decided to build my own. During my development journey, I discovered several other clipboard managers while researching solutions for my feature ideas.

Here are some other clipboard managers I recommend you take a look at to see which fits your workflow better.
- [Maccy](https://maccy.app) - Set your price, recommended $12
- [Clipy](https://clipy-app.com) - Free
- [Paste](https://pasteapp.io) - $29.99/year subscription
